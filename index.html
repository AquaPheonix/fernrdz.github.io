Bio

With 6+ years of grassroots esports experience and almost 3 years of professional experience that started with his love for Super Smash Bros. Melee, Fernando is proud to present his portfolio detailing his rich history in esports and the many communities that pride themselves on competition and unity through the games they play.

Fernando Rodriguez is an esports broadcaster and tournament admin that handles shows and clients of all sizes throughout various esports titles including Super Smash Bros. Melee, Guilty Gear -Strive- and NBA2K. Fernando prides himself on the scope of his work and on producing quality shows and content for any event, no matter the size or title. In addition, Fernando has also worked with other entities in various other roles, including the FIFA eMLS league as a tournament admin and Tourney Locator as both a tournament admin and broadcast technician.
